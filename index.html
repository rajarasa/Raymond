Fabrics

Raymond is one of the largest vertically and horizontally integrated manufacturers of worsted suiting fabric in the world and commands a dominant market share of over 60 percent in the worsted suiting fabric space in India. Raymond’s state-of-the- art fabric manufacturing plants have an aggregate manufacturing capacity of 38 million metres of suiting fabric extending across all wool, poly- wool, silk, polyester viscose blend, cotton blend, linen blend and other premium blends. Marketed under the brand ‘Raymond Fine Fabrics’, it is undoubtedly among the most preferred brands in the Textile sector.

Raymond manufactures some of the finest shirting fabrics in India, the unit produces high value cotton and linen shirting and bottom weight fabrics for leading domestic and international brands.
